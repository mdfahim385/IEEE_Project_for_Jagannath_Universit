About IEEE

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers is a professional association for electronic engineering and electrical engineering dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE is the complete package to technology devotees trying their heart out to bring their dream technologies to come to life. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. As the world’s largest technical professional association, IEEE’s membership has long been composed of engineers, scientists, and allied professionals. These include computer scientists, software developers, information technology professionals, physicists, medical doctors, and many others in addition to IEEE’s electrical and electronics engineering core. For this reason, the organization no longer goes by the full name, except on legal business documents, and is referred to simply as IEEE. There are more than 420,000 IEEE members in more than 160 countries. IEEE publishes a third of the world’s technical literature in electrical engineering, computer science and electronics and is a leading developer of international standards that underpin many of today's telecommunications, information technology and power generation products and services.


About IEEE Bangladesh Section

IEEE Bangladesh Section is one of the finest and most popular local sections by member growth and activities among all other sections around the world founded on 20th November 1993. The section lies under Region-10 (Asia-Pacific). This Region 10 was created on 1st January 1967 and at present, it has 56 Sections, 5 Councils, 8 Sub-sections, 334 Chapters, and 490 student branches. With a membership of over 67,500, it is one of the largest regions in IEEE. Currently, the IEEE Bangladesh Section has over 3,500+ members, 51 student branches, 09 Society Chapters and 02 Affinity Groups. Also, the section has 03 SIGHT groups (One section SIGHT group and two student branch SIGHT groups). The Section has been actively organizing many conferences, congress, workshops, seminars and as well as webinars and short courses on different topics of Electrical, Electronic, Communication and Computer engineering. Delegates from renowned industry and Professors of different universities conduct these sessions. It helps students to be introduced with the industrial environment and makes them expert for the upcoming challenges.


About IEEE JNU Student Branch

IEEE JNU Student Branch is a student run organization that has been officially started its journey at 24th October 2017. Currently the branch has more than 30 student members and the geo-code of this branch is STB 01037. IEEE JNU (Jagannath University) STUDENT BRANCH was created for the students of JNU to get familiar with IEEE and the benefits it is offering. IEEE JNU Student Branch is raising operative student branch among all the IEEE student branch sections in Bangladesh. With high spirited members and experienced mentors, IEEE JNU Student Branch operates to promote IEEE and become a prominent student branch. We give our best for the enhancement of the students to develop as a technical as well as professional individual. Through workshops, talks, conferences, and other events, IEEE members enhance and distribute their array of skills. IEEE JNU Student Branch offers students different seminars, courses, workshops, technical tours and other to develop their ability to grow as a professional so that the students can dedicate themselves to the prosperity of humanity.
